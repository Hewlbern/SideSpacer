We are always expanding and new locations are the lifeblood of our growth.

If you think that your restaurant or retail space would fit SideSpacer well,
we would like to hear from you!

Our formula has been tested and adjusted,
so here are some things we have learned which
are important for the success of a new location
on the SideSpacer.

  • Closed during the day to patrons (if it's a restaurant)
  • Seats at least 80 people (ideally more)
  • Beautiful, interesting interior
  • Great natural light
  • Convenient street level access
  • Accessible by public transportation or is in a highly trafficked area

If you think your space meets these criteria, tell us about it by filling out the form.
